# AI Study Planner – Logic

## Input
- Subjects
- Exam dates
- Daily available study hours

## Processing
1. Analyze subject priority based on deadlines
2. Distribute study hours evenly
3. Allocate time for revision and breaks

## Output
A structured daily/weekly study plan that helps students manage time effectively.

## Sample Output

Monday:
- Mathematics – 2 hours
- Science – 1.5 hours
- Revision – 30 minutes
- Break after every 50 minutes
